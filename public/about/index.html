About us

At NoteMaps we believe in simple, easy to use design. We created NoteMaps to be able to make sense of the information we had in front of me. We needed more than just simple pieces of paper. We needed to see the connections between them. NoteMaps allows you to take your notes and draw connections between them in a mindmap like style.

Our mission is to help the world make sense of the information thats out there. It doesn't matter if its ideas for presents or a complex work project. If you're struggling to see the big picture or get a sense of how it all fits together, then NoteMaps is for you.
