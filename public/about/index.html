About us

At NoteMaps we believe in simple, easy to use design. We created NoteMaps to be able to make sense of the information we had in front of us. We needed more than just simple pieces of paper, we needed to see the connections between them. NoteMaps allows you to do this by taking your notes and drawing connections between them in a mindmap like style.

Our mission is to help the world make sense of the information that's out there. It doesn't matter if it's ideas for presents or a complex work project. If you're struggling to see the big picture or get a sense of how it all fits together, then NoteMaps is for you.
